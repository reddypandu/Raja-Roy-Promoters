About Navya Mile Stone

Navya Milestone is our New Residential Layout in Sadashivapet @ Kamkole, Sangareddy Hyderabad on very close vicinity to Mumbai National Highway (Nh65) with modern amenities and is in proximity to 13000 Acres NIMZ Project, Woxsen University, IIT Hyderabad & MRF Factory near Mumbai national highway (NH65) which is the fastest growing investor’s hub and is a home to several projects like Pepsi Co., Aurobindo Pharma, Kirby Toshiba, PebsPennar, MRF tyres, NIMZ, WOXSEN etc

With a focus on sustainability and community, Navya Milestone Project creates spaces that inspire harmony and well-being. From meticulously crafted residences to vibrant recreational areas, every aspect is tailored to elevate the quality of life for its residents. Embark on a journey of contemporary living with Navya Milestone Project where every detail is crafted to perfection, promising a life of fulfillment and prosperity.

Navya Milestone Project sets a new standard in modern urban living, where architectural brilliance meets unparalleled comfort. This visionary real estate venture offers a diverse range of residential options, from sleek apartments to spacious villas, catering to various lifestyles and preferences. Nestled in prime locations, each unit boasts breathtaking views and convenient access to essential amenities, ensuring a life of convenience and luxury.

About Mango Farm House

Mango Farm Home is our New Project in Sadashivpet, @ China Chelmeda Sangareddy Hyderabad on very close vicinity to Mumbai National Highway (NH65) with modern amenities and is in proximity to 13000 acres NIMZ Project, Woxsen University, IIT Hyderabad & MRF Factory near Mumbai national highway (NH65) which is the fastest growing investor's hub and is a home to several projects like Pepsi Co. Aurobindo Pharma, Kirby Toshiba, PebsPennar, MRF tyre, NIMZ, WOXSEN etc.

Designed to harmonize with the natural landscape, each farmhouse in the Mango Farm House Project is a testament to elegance and comfort. Whether it's a cozy weekend getaway or a permanent residence, residents can expect nothing short of opulence and sophistication. With thoughtfully crafted interiors and modern amenities, every aspect of the farmhouse is tailored to enhance the quality of life and provide a sanctuary for relaxation and rejuvenation.
